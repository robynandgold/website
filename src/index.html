Jewellery with a past, chosen for your future.

Robyn & Gold was born from a quiet obsession with jewellery that feels like it has lived a life before yours. We work with trusted auction houses and private collections to source antique and vintage rings worth passing on.

Every piece is carefully assessed, lightly restored only where necessary, and photographed without filters so you can see what we see: patina, character and that elusive, old-world sparkle.

Based in Ireland

Each ring is shipped from Ireland in fully insured packaging, with a handwritten note detailing its story, metal and stones. We are happy to answer questions about sizing, styling and care before you purchase.

Why antique?

Craftsmanship first

Before mass production, jewellers carved, engraved and set each stone by hand. That care is still visible today in crisp settings, balanced proportions and fine details.

Responsible luxury

Choosing antique extends the life of existing gold and diamonds, reducing the need for new mining while still offering something truly special.

Thoughtfully curated

Each Robyn & Gold piece is carefully selected in small quantities. No mass production, just a considered collection of rings with real character.
